FAQ

Common questions

Does this require WiFi or internet at the property?▼
No. Our sensors use a dedicated wireless network (IoT) that connects to our hub via cellular. No tenant WiFi, no IT configuration, no passwords to share.
How long does installation take?▼
About 2 minutes per sensor. Stick it under the sink, behind the toilet, or near the water heater. No tools, no drilling, no wiring.
What happens when a leak is detected?▼
You get a text and email in under 60 seconds. If you have smart shutoff valves installed, we can kill the water automatically. No waiting for a tenant to notice.
How much does water damage actually cost?▼
The average insurance claim for water damage is $10,000+. Undetected leaks can easily cause $50k+ in structural damage. Our service costs less than a coffee per unit per month.
Can I try it before committing?▼
Absolutely. We offer a free pilot program — we'll install sensors in 5 units and you can see the system in action. No commitment, no credit card.
