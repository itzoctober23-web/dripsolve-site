FAQ

Common questions

What sensors do I need?▼
We work with Tuya-compatible WiFi sensors — the most common and affordable type. Most are $10-18 on Amazon. They connect over WiFi so no hub needed. After you purchase, we send you the exact links and setup guide.
Do I need WiFi or internet at the property?▼
Yes, sensors connect to WiFi. If a property doesn't have WiFi, a $30 hub with cellular backup solves it. We walk you through everything.
How hard is setup?▼
About 10 minutes per sensor. Stick it down, scan the QR code with our app, done. We provide a video walkthrough.
Can I try it before committing?▼
Yes — free 14-day trial. No credit card required. You just need one compatible sensor to test with.
What if the internet goes down?▼
Sensors store data locally and sync when connection is restored. For critical properties, we recommend the cellular backup hub.
