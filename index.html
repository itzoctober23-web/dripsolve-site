FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to buy sensors or hardware?▼
Nope. Everything is included in the monthly price. We bring, install, and maintain all equipment. If something breaks, we replace it at no cost.
How long does setup take?▼
Usually 1-2 days for a typical building. We walk through the property, place sensors in high-risk areas, configure the dashboard, and test everything. You're live by end of day 2.
What happens when a leak is detected?▼
You get a text and email immediately. Depending on your plan, we also call you directly. We can also alert your maintenance team or shutoff valve system if integrated.
Is there a contract?▼
No long-term contracts. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. We're confident you'll stay because the service pays for itself the first time it catches a leak.
What areas do you serve?▼
We currently operate in TX, CA, AZ, FL, and GA. Expanding to more states based on demand.
